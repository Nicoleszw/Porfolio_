Project Overview

MindMood is an AI‑driven mental‑wellness chat platform that allows users to express their emotions, receive empathetic responses, and record daily mood data. The application stores mood entries in a PostgreSQL database and visualises trends over time through a dedicated dashboard. Built during my full‑stack bootcamp, MindMood focuses on an intuitive interface and privacy‑first architecture.
